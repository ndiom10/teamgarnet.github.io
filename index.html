AI and Deskilling in Education

A research project exploring whether artificial intelligence enhances learning or quietly weakens critical academic skills through overreliance, cognitive offloading, and automation.

View Findings Learn More

Abstract

This project examines how AI tools are changing student learning, writing, reasoning, and confidence. Through comparative research and meta-analysis, Team Garnet investigates whether AI shifts skill distribution productively or contributes to deskilling and skill erosion.
